The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time

Basic Information

An ever-continuing prophecy that foretells a monumental battle between forces of good and evil; forces that fight for the same three virtues: Courage, Power, and Wisdom, forces that could change the scope of their world forever.
It is said that the hero of this procephy takes on his role when he dreams of this harrowing scenario.
In the Kokiri Forest, a sacred land in the kingdom of Hyrule, a group of forest children named the Kokiri aided by fairies, existed a young boy by the name of Link, who would soon become familiar with his destiny.
Sensing that the boy had begrudgingly became aware of his right path in the world, the heart of the Kokiri Forest, the Great Deku Tree, sends out a fairy by the name of Navi to wake up the young boy and to inform him of his goal: to prevent that prophetic force of evil from ripping the three virtues of the world from its very core.
